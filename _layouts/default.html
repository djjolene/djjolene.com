DJ Jolene has been rocking NYC with her blend of deep sexy house music for over a decade. She is the founder of the House channel for the popular Digitally Imported internet radio station and has also taught regularly at New York's Scratch DJ Academy. Her driving, bass-heavy mixes are influenced by the cities in which she has resided: London, San Francisco and New York.
